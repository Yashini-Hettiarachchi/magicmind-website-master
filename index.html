Research Purpose

This website serves as a comprehensive platform for documenting our research on Non-Verbal Learning Disorder (NVLD). We aim to:

  • Document our research journey and findings
  • Share project milestones and achievements
  • Engage with stakeholders and the academic community
  • Provide accessible resources for understanding NVLD
